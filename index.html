About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at Nankai University.

My current interests center on reinforcement learning, robot learning, and embodied intelligence. I am especially interested in how learning-based methods can help robots perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.

Outside research and study, I enjoy guitar, sports, fitness, and meeting people who are curious about intelligent systems.
