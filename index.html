About Me

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at Nankai University.

My research interests lie in reinforcement learning, robot learning, and embodied intelligence. I am interested in how intelligent systems perceive, learn, and interact with the physical world.

I also maintain a personal blog for study notes, paper readings, and learning records at dpthink.vercel.app.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement learning and decision making
  • Robot learning, policy learning, and embodied intelligence
  • Vision-language-action models and action generation
  • Generative models for robot policies, including diffusion and flow-based methods
